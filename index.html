Crate Adventure Pvt. Ltd identified the gap in Sri Lanka between general tourism and adventure tourism. Most / travelers now seek to spent time with nature and experience adventure. Majority of the tourists that arrive in Sri Lanka are once that want to keep a foot forward towards adventure. They seek activities which has now become a sport such as ATV riding which is mostly available in the North American and Europe a region. This gap was the key to initiate Crate Adventure. Joining-hands with the World's leading ATV manufacturer, a North America Company, Can Am by BRP; Crate Adventure will implement ATV tracks according to global expertise from professionals, Crate Adventure will feature top in the class, high-end ATVs that exceeds tourist expectation.
